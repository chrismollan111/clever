Do you require a bank account for your business?

We have partnered with two great banking services to provide you with the best of the online banking sector.  Both banks offer quick turnaround times (with Tide offering 5 minute account set up) and free banking (restrictions apply) and we are able to liaise with both on your behalf to get you set up quickly and efficiently. 
Tide is an alternative bank account aimed at small businesses, freelancers, and independents.  It's app & desktop based and the application process, if you have a driving licence or passport handy, usually takes as little as 5 minutes to get set up via the app - ideal for clients wanting a quick turnaround!  You can see more information regarding the bank here.  As a partner of Tide bank, Clever Accounts will start the application process for you if you select this option, which means you will benefit from free banking for the first 6 months.
Even better, you can connect to Tide via our Open Banking function to allow seamless download of banking transactions.
Cater Allen is a more 'traditional' bank account aimed at small businesses, freelancers, and independents.  Application processing times are approx 2-3 weeks from sign up and you must complete a paper application plus provide additional identification.  You can see more information regarding the bank here.  As a partner of Cater Allen, Clever Accounts will start the application process for you if you select this option, which means you will benefit for free banking.

Where do you want your registered office address?

We can act as your registered office address if you would like, free of charge. This will stop your home address (if you do not have a business address) from appearing on the Companies House website and also means any correspondence from Companies House will be posted directly to us. If you do choose our address as registered office, we do require you to enter a "business address" - typically your home address, for HMRC purposes however.
Registered Office Address - enter the details of the registered office to use e.g. business or home address.
Business Trading Address - enter the details of the trading address.  The trading address could be a personal home address.
